About LOCAL Hospital. Co. Za

Founded in 1999, LOCAL Hospital. Co. Za was built on a simple promise: world-class care close to home. Today we serve thousands of patients each month with more than 300 staff across clinical and support teams.

Our mission is to deliver safe, respectful, and equitable healthcare. Our vision is a healthier community empowered by access, education, and prevention.

“Every patient is a person first.”
